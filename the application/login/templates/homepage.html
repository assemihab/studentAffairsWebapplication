Welcome to Giza University Student Affairs

Giza University is a new university in Cairo, Egypt. Founded in 2023, it is the newest university in Egypt and one of the largest in the country. Giza University offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across various disciplines, including engineering, medicine, humanities, social sciences, and more.

At Giza University, we are committed to providing our students with a world-class education that prepares them for successful careers and leadership roles in their communities and beyond.
